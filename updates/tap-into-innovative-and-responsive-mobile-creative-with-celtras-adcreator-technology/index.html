Tap Into Innovative and Responsive Mobile Creative with Celtra’s AdCreator technology

Hotmob Updates Oct 31, 2016

Introducing a brand new set of mobile creative demo presented by Hotmob creative team. These mobile creatives offer brands and advertisers innovative and responsive ad designs to enhance user engagement. Thanks to our partner, Celtra for the mobile-friendly ad creative tool ⎼ “AdCreator” with advanced HTML5 mobile ad development technology. More than 250 components and features can be applied across devices, such as drag and drop, auto-play inline video player or even complex animations.

 

Advertisers can be assured that their mobile creatives to be delivered across multiple screen sizes. Celtra’s AdCreator technology is granted accreditation by the Media Rating Council (MRC) for the measurement of served ad impressions and ad engagements across desktop, mobile web and mobile in-app environments. Its range of technology products and measurement standards has adhered to the guidelines of the Interactive Advertising Bureau (IAB) which set the industry standard of quality digital campaign.

 

The following mobile creative demos were developed by Hotmob creative team with a combination of various components, features and animations, which can be fully customizable and responsive to multiple screen sizes.
