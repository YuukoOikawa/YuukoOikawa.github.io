About Us

A leader in mobile marketing, Hotmob operates the largest hybrid mobile marketplace in the region, delivering integrated and performance driven mobile marketing solutions with the facilitation of proprietary advertising technologies, robust ad-serving platforms and creative media that are engineered to meet the growing demand from businesses across Asia.

Hotmob drives outstanding ROI for advertisers by targeting the customer segmentation through suitable mobile platforms; engaging them with innovative ad formats and interactions; and measuring campaign success against key performance indicators to demonstrate Hotmob’s value-add and service commitment.

Hotmob helps media and app publishers to strategically create mobile user experiences that delight users, and generate sustainable revenue for their businesses – from functionality, context, user input, content, marketing to user satisfaction evaluation.

Hotmob offers a one-stop solution for advertisers and app publishers, including ad design, planning, implementation, serving, tracking and reporting. Our award-winning ad-serving infrastructure – Mobile Advertising Marketplace (MAM) help affiliates to build, manage and measure mobile campaigns across multiple mobile platforms.
